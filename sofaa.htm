Modern Living Room Curved Sofa

Minimalist&Modern, Add More Charm To Your Life!

MODERN DESIGN: Our 4 Seater curved Sofa features warm color, elegant curves and clean lines give it a stylish appearance to brings modern style and fashion touch to your room space.

Curve Sofa

Designed with ergonomic curved backrest and armrest, allow you to lean or lay on the sofa for supportive comfort. It is very suitable for relaxing on this sofa couch.

Comfortable Seat Cushion

The seating cushion is filled with thicken high-density sponge. The inside pocket spring increases elasticity of the seat. Come with 3 pillows, the couch provides you an enjoyable sitting experience.

Perfect Size

Overall size: 48.8"D x 101.4"W x 26.9"H. This minimalist sofa is the ideal addition to any living room. Perfect for small space, bedroom, apartment and home office.
